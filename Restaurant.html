Welcome to Sukhshree Restaurant

Sukhshree Pure Veg, located in Satara on Bangalore Highway, is a delightful establishment offering a fantastic food experience. With no waiting period, prompt seating, and a warm ambiance, it's perfect for family gatherings. The restaurant is known for its wonderful spread of Gujarati and Rajasthani vegetarian dishes that are both delectable and diverse. Visitors praise the variety of daily-changing dishes available and highlight the unlimited Rajasthani thali as a must-try.
