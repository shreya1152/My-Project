Established in 2011, Yashoda Technical Campus, Faculty of Engineering, Satara, has emerged as one of the leading institutions for technical and professional education in the region. It is managed by Yashoda Shikshan Prasarak Mandal (YSPM), Satara, which is committed to transforming aspiring youth into competent professionals through a holistic educational approach. The institute is approved by the All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE), New Delhi, and the Directorate of Technical Education (DTE), Maharashtra. Engineering programs are affiliated with Dr. Babasaheb Ambedkar Technological University (DBATU), Lonere. Two programs at the campus are accredited by the National Board of Accreditation (NBA), and the institution is accredited by the National Assessment and Accreditation Council (NAAC) with a B+ grade. YTC provides a vibrant academic environment supported by advanced infrastructure, modern laboratories, digital classrooms, and dedicated spaces for innovation and student development. The institution focuses on delivering industry-oriented training, hands-on learning, entrepreneurship development, and soft skills enhancement through regular workshops, internships, and live projects. A dynamic Training and Placement Cell ensures strong industry linkage and excellent career opportunities for students. Beyond academics, the campus fosters student engagement through cultural, technical, and sports activities, as well as community outreach programs. Through its commitment to academic excellence, practical skill development, and ethical values, YTC aims to shape aesthetically sensitive, socially responsible, and technologically competent engineers and management professionals equipped to meet global challenges.

Mission

Yashoda Technical Campus aspires to be a role model for institutional excellence and to emerge as a Leader in Technical & Management Education and Research by providing value based Quality education with World class infrastructure and strengthening strategic linkage with Industry, Premier Institutions and thereby fostering excellence in teaching, research and community service to meet educational, social and economic needs of the region and the nation to create human society.

Vision

To provide opportunities to students and faculty to acquire higher qualification and experience with continuous learning opportunities. To impart quality education by implementing state-of-the-art teaching learning methods to enrich the academic competency, credibility and integrity of the students. To empower the students with current knowledge, skills and right attitude in order to meet the challenges of future & to develop competence towards serving the ever changing needs of Industry and Society. To create and maintain a vibrant research environment to promote excellence in research and extension activities.
