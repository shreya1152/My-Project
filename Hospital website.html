About Hospital

A hospital is a healthcare institution that provides comprehensive medical services, including diagnosis, treatment, and rehabilitation, for both inpatients and outpatients. Hospitals are equipped with specialized staff, advanced medical equipment, and facilities to offer a wide range of healthcare services. They also play a crucial role in medical education, research, and disease prevention.
