  1. how do I reset my password?

    You can reset your password by clicking on "Forgot Password" on the login page and following the instructions

    • Use a strong password with special characters
    • Check your email spam folder for reset link

  2. what if I can't access my account?

    If you're unable to log in, review the issue and apply the suggested solution below:

    Issue solution
    Incorrect password Use the "Forgot Password" option to reset it
    Email not verified Check your inbox and click on the verification link
