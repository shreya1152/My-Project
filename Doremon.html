Doraemon is a popular Japanese manga and anime series about a robotic cat from the future named Doraemon, who travels back in time to help a young boy named Nobita Nobi. Created by Fujiko Fujio, the series first appeared in 1969 and has since become a global phenomenon. Doraemon uses gadgets from his four-dimensional pocket to assist Nobita, often leading to humorous and heartwarming adventures.Doraemon is a robotic cat from the 22nd century, sent back in time to help Nobita Nobi, a clumsy and unlucky boy, improve his life. The series, created by Fujiko F. Fujio, explores themes of friendship, perseverance, and personal growth as Doraemon uses gadgets from his four-dimensional pocket to assist Nobita. While the manga never had an official ending, the core story focuses on Doraemon's mission to ensure Nobita secures a better future for his descendants.

Nobita Nobi is a fictional character and one of the main characters in the Doraemon anime and manga series created by Fujiko Fujio. Known as Sidney, Specky and Noby in some English localizations of the anime, Nobita is usually depicted as an elementary school student.

Shizuka Minamoto, often called Shizu-chan or Shizuka-chan, is a central character in the Japanese manga and anime series Doraemon. She is depicted as a kind, smart, and pretty girl who is one of Nobita's close friends and his love interest.

Nobita has three main friends: Takeshi Goda (nicknamed Gian), Suneo Honekawa (Gian's sidekick), and Shizuka Minamoto, Nobita's best friend and love-interest. Gian is a strong, leading and domineering boy, but also loyal to his friends.

Suneo Honekawa, known as Sneech in some English dubs, is a main character in the Doraemon series. He is a rich and spoiled kid who enjoys showing off his possessions to his friends, often to make them jealous, especially Nobita.
