School Cultural Fest 2025

Introduction of Cultural Fest

The Cultural Fest 2025 is an exciting day filled with music, drama, dance, and creativity! Students from all grades participate and showcase their talents in various cultural events.Participants must report 15 minutes before their event. Respect time limits and follow instructions from event coordinators. Plagiarism or copied performances will lead to disqualification.
